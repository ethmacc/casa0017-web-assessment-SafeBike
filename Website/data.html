Data Sources

The project primarily utilized data from the MPS Recorded Crime dataset, broken down by Lower Layer Super Output Areas (LSOAs). This dataset comprises of incident counts of all types of crimes over a two year period. To ensure its relevance to cyclists and bike owners, our team carried out significant preprocessing before visualising the data.

Data Processing

Heavy duty data-science tools such as Python and its fantastic libraries were used by our team to filter out irrelevant crime data. Our team then merged the filtered dataset with the GIS boundaries of each LSOA. The final merged dataset forms the basis of this web application's data-driven backend.
